Services

RESIDENTIAL

Foundations for House Extensions

London Screw Piling enables fast, low disruption foundations for house extensions using helical screw piles that install quickly without heavy excavation or concrete.

This means less mess, faster build times, and reliable structural support, even in tight urban spaces or poor ground conditions.

The system is fully engineered, load tested, and compliant with building regulations for peace of mind.

RESTRICTED ACCESS

Handheld Foundations

London Screw Piling provides handheld installation systems designed for sites with limited access, such as basements, gardens, or enclosed spaces.

Compact equipment can be carried through standard doorways and installed with minimal noise and disruption.

This eliminates the need for large machinery or excavation while still delivering high capacity, structurally sound foundations.

UNDERPINNING

Subsidence Repairs

London Screw Piling stabilises and repairs subsiding structures using screw pile underpinning, transferring building loads to deeper, stable ground.

The system installs quickly with minimal vibration, no spoil removal, and immediate load bearing capability.

That makes it a cost effective, low disruption solution for restoring structural integrity in affected properties.

HOUSING

New Build Foundations

London Screw Piling delivers efficient, fully engineered foundations for new build housing using high capacity helical piles.

These systems install faster than traditional methods, require no curing time, and perform reliably across varying soil conditions.

That helps developers save time, reduce costs, and build with confidence on challenging sites.

COMMERCIAL

Utilities Foundations

London Screw Piling supports commercial and infrastructure projects with robust foundation systems for utilities such as telecoms, power, and civil works.

Our screw pile solutions offer rapid installation, high load capacity, and minimal environmental impact.

That makes them ideal for time sensitive projects requiring precision and reliability.

ECOLOGICAL

Tree Safe Foundations

London Screw Piling enables construction near trees with non invasive, root friendly foundation systems.

Screw piles are installed with minimal soil disturbance, avoiding damage to root protection zones while still delivering strong structural support.

That makes them ideal for environmentally sensitive or regulated sites.
